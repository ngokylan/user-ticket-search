Heading component provides styling of typography based on Design System.

Sizes
```js
<Heading htmlTag="h1" type="title" size="xl">Title Extra Large</Heading>
<Heading htmlTag="h2" type="title" size="lg">Title Large</Heading>
<Heading htmlTag="h3" type="title" size="rg">Title Regular</Heading>
<Heading htmlTag="h4" type="title" size="sm">Title Small</Heading>
<Heading htmlTag="h5" type="title" size="xs">Title Extra Small</Heading>
<Heading htmlTag="h6" type="title" size="xxs">Title Micro</Heading>
<Heading htmlTag="h1" type="heading" size="rg">Heading</Heading>
<Heading htmlTag="h2" type="heading" size="sm">Heading Small</Heading>
<Heading htmlTag="h3" type="heading" size="xs">Heading Extra Small</Heading>
<Heading htmlTag="h4" type="heading" size="xxs">Heading Micro</Heading>
```

Alignment
```js
<Heading align="left">Title - left</Heading>
<Heading align="center">Title - Center </Heading>
<Heading align="right">Title - Right </Heading>
<Heading align="justify">Title - Justify </Heading>
```

Decoration
```js
<Heading decoration="overline">Title - Overline </Heading>
<Heading decoration="lineThrough">Title - Line Through </Heading>
<Heading decoration="underline">Title - Underline </Heading>
```

Transform
```js
<Heading transform="lowercase">Title - Lowercase </Heading>
<Heading transform="uppercase">Title - Uppercase </Heading>
<Heading transform="capitalize">Title - Capitalize </Heading>
```

Bold, Italic, Truncate
```js
<Heading isBold>Title - Bold </Heading>
<Heading isItalic>Title - Italic </Heading>
<Heading isTruncate>Title title title title title title title title title title  title title title title title title title title title  title title title title title title title title title - Truncate </Heading>
```

Colour
```js
<Heading color="primary">Title Primary</Heading>
<Heading color="secondary">Title Secondary</Heading>
<Heading color="information">Title Information</Heading>
<Heading color="warning">Title Warning</Heading>
<Heading color="success">Title Success</Heading>
<Heading color="danger">Title Danger</Heading>
```
